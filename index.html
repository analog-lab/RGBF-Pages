[ ABOUT ] ────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RGBFILM.TXT

RGB Film faithfully simulates vintage film photography from the 1930s to 2020s. Every decade has its own unique character — grain, color palette, scanlines, interlace, and more.


All effects are processed in real-time through Metal GPU acceleration, delivering a live viewfinder experience that feels like looking through a vintage camera. The entire interface is built with an authentic DOS aesthetic — because great photography deserves a great interface.

[ FEATURES ] ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
FILM SIMULATION

11 decade presets with LUT color grading, overlay effects, and per-era audio processing. Each preset captures the authentic feel of its era.

REAL-TIME GPU

Metal compute shaders power the entire filter pipeline — zero-lag live preview with CRT distortion, scanlines, interlace, and glitch effects.

BEAUTY FILTER

AI face landmark detection with Vision framework. Adjustable cheek, jaw, and chin slimming with smooth, jitter-free real-time processing.

DOS INTERFACE

4 authentic retro themes — Phosphor, Amber, Arctic, and Pop. Full DOS-style UI with custom components, scanline overlays, and CRT frame.

PHOTO & VIDEO

Capture photos and record video with all filters applied in real-time. H.264 encoding with EXIF metadata and GPS location stamping.

MULTILINGUAL

Full localization in English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese Simplified and Traditional. Per-language pixel fonts for authentic display.
